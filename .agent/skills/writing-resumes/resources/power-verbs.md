# Resume Power Verbs

Use these to start your bullet points for maximum impact.

### Management & Leadership
- **Spearheaded**: Lead a project from conception to completion.
- **Orchestrated**: Coordinated complex movements or processes.
- **Mentored**: Highlights team growth and interpersonal impact.

### Technical & Execution
- **Architected**: Built systems or frameworks from the ground up.
- **Implemented**: Brought a plan or design into action.
- **Optimized**: Improved performance, speed, or efficiency.
- **Automated**: Replaced manual tasks with software solutions.

### Results & Achievement
- **Attained**: Reached a specific goal or milestone.
- **Surpassed**: Went beyond expectations or previous records.
- **Quantified**: Put a number to an achievement (e.g., "$2M in savings").
- **Revitalized**: Brought new life to a failing or stagnant process.
